Kaede Polkinghorne

I am a multidisciplinary designer presently thinking about blood and public history. With professional experience in participatory design, landscape architecture, and public sector planning across the gulf south, my research focuses on how stories take shape and are remembered along our third coast. I am currently pursuing a master's in city planning at MIT.
